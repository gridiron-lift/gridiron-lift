Veterans Scholarship

Helping our Heroes Build Successful New Careers in Real Estate.

About the Scholarship

The Colibri Veterans Scholarship is our way of giving back to those who have given so much to our country. In partnership with the Wounded Warrior Project and The Bob Woodruff Foundation, we will be giving away 1,000 scholarships — 500 from each partner — to help veterans further their education, launch new careers, and pave the path to fulfilling futures.

Veterans receiving these scholarships will get access to their choice of learning package (in livestream or self-paced format) on Real Estate Express. Additionally, Colibri will ensure each recipients' success by hosting a quarterly livestream veterans forum to allow any current students to meet and ask questions from our lead instructor.

What's included in the scholarship?

Colibri’s Real Estate Express (REX) is the country's leading online school for real estate and broker licensing education. Recipients of this scholarship will get access to the Real Estate Express Ultimate learning package. It includes required pre-licensing real estate education courses, state-approved instructor support, exam prep, exam cram webinar and video, real estate dictionary, instructor Q&A, printed course textbook, quarterly livestream support, and a one year professional development membership.

What can recipients expect from the scholarship?

This scholarship helps veterans meet their state’s real estate licensing requirements and prepares them to take the licensing exams to become real estate agents.

REX has helped millions of students start flourishing new careers, and this scholarship program provides a great opportunity for veterans to achieve this milestone with zero out of pocket expense for their education.

How are winners selected?

Each foundation will send a list of 500 winners to Colibri. In total, 1,000 recipients will receive a welcome email with a link to the Real Estate Express website, along with a promo code to redeem their free course.

How do winners redeem their free course?

Winners will visit the scholarship landing page via the link sent to them in the congratulatory email. From there, they will be directed to a private landing page on the Real Estate Express website, where they will select their state and package, then proceed to checkout to waive the fee and register for their course.

How do winners start their course?

After redeeming their promo code and completing registration, winners will be able to access and start their course from their student dashboard.

Where is this scholarship available?

The scholarship is only available in the 38 states where Real Estate Express and their sister companies offer online education: ​​AL, AK, AZ, AR, CA, CO, FL, GA, HI, IL, IN, IO, KS, KY, LA, MD, MA, MN, MI, MS, MO, MT, NE, NV, NJ, NY, OH, OK, PA, SC, TN, TX, VT, VA, WA, DC, WI, and WY.

What if Real Estate Express is not available in my state?

If you live in a state where one of our sister brands offer courses (AZ, OH, NC, NV), we will contact you to help you get set up with an equivalent course/learning package.

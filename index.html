The Gridiron Veterans Scholarship

Helping Veterans Launch Successful Careers in Real Estate.

About the Scholarship

The Gridiron Veterans Scholarship is our way of giving back to those who have given so much to our country. Gridiron Capital and Colibri are teaming up with Wounded Warrior Project® and The Bob Woodruff Foundation to offer 1,000 of America’s veterans a life-changing opportunity to forge a successful new career in the real estate sector.

What's included in the scholarship?

Colibri Real Estate is the country's leading online school for real estate and broker licensing education. Recipients of this scholarship will get access to the Colibri Real Estate Ultimate learning package. It includes required pre-licensing real estate education courses, state-approved instructor support, exam prep, exam cram webinar and video, real estate dictionary, instructor Q&A, printed course textbook, quarterly livestream support, and a one-year professional development membership.

What can recipients expect from the scholarship?

This scholarship helps veterans meet their state’s real estate licensing requirements and prepares them to take the licensing exams to become real estate agents.

Colibri Real Estate has helped millions of students start flourishing new careers, and this scholarship program provides a great opportunity for veterans to achieve this milestone with zero out-of-pocket expense for their education.

How are winners selected?

Each foundation will send a list of 500 winners to Colibri Real Estate. In total, 1,000 recipients will receive a welcome email with a link to the Colibri Real Estate website, along with a promo code to redeem their free course.

How do winners redeem their free course?

Winners will visit the scholarship landing page via the link sent to them in the congratulatory email. From there, they will be directed to a private landing page on the Colibri Real Estate website, where they will select their state and package, then proceed to checkout to waive the fee and register for their course.

Winners in AZ, NC, and OH will contact Colibri Real Estate to receive a promo code and link to the corresponding sister brand website, where they will be able to redeem the code.

How do winners start their course?

After redeeming their promo code and completing registration, winners will be able to access and start their course from their student dashboard.

Where is this scholarship available?

The scholarship is only available in the 38 states where Colibri Real Estate and their sister companies offer online education: ​​AL, AK, AZ, AR, CA, CO, DC, FL, GA, HI, IL, IN, IA, KS, KY, LA, MD, MA, MI, MN, MS, MO, MT, NE, NV, NJ, NY, OH, OK, PA, SC, TN, TX, VT, VA, WA, WI, and WY.

If you live in a state where one of our sister brands offers courses (AZ, NC, OH), you can contact veteranscholarships@colibrigroup.com and we will help you get set up with an equivalent course/learning package.

What if Colibri Real Estate is not available in my state?

Unfortunately, if Colibri Real Estate or sister brand courses are not available in your state, you will not be eligible for the scholarship.
